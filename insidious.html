Synopsis

Josh Lemport, a high school teacher, and his wife, Lanna, a freelance musician, have a happy family of five. But since they moved to their new home, panic and anxiety followed. Infant daughter Kelly often cries for no apparent reason, second son Foster is terrified of the bedroom, and eldest son Dalton falls into a prolonged coma after a fright. After this, all kinds of strange and terrifying events happened one after another. The Lemberts were so overwhelmed that they nearly broke down, and they finally decided to move out of the ominous place.
But it didn't end there. The devil who infested them seemed to follow the Lempot family to their new residence, and even threatened to occupy Dalton's body.
